About

I build websites the way I'd want to hire someone to build mine.

I'm Aiden — the person behind ABJ Design Co. I started this studio because too many good businesses get stuck choosing between an agency price tag and a site that looks like everyone else's template.

Process

From first message to launch

1

Discovery call

A short conversation about your business, your goals for the site, and your budget and timeline.

2

Proposal & agreement

You'll get a clear proposal with pricing and scope, plus a simple contract to sign before we begin.

3

Design

I design your pages and walk you through them before any code is written, so changes are easy and cheap.

4

Build & review

Your site is built and you get rounds of feedback built into your package to get it right.

5

Launch

Your site goes live on your domain, with a walkthrough of how to make basic updates yourself.

Why ABJ Design Co. exists

ABJ Design Co. is built to be the option in between — real design work and hand-built code, without the markup that comes from a big agency's overhead. It's a small studio, which means you're talking directly to the person actually designing and building your site, from the first conversation through launch.

The name comes from my initials — Aiden Basil Jajo. That means no account managers or handoffs between departments — just one person who knows your project from start to finish.
