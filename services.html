Good to know

A few common questions

What's not included in the base price?

Domain registration and hosting are billed directly to you by the provider (usually $15–25/year for a domain). I'll help you set both up at no extra charge.

How do payments work?

A 50% deposit to begin, and the remaining 50% due at launch. Milestone-based payment plans are available for the Business and E-commerce packages.

What if I need more pages or features later?

You can add pages, features, or a maintenance plan any time — no need to start a whole new project.
